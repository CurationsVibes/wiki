# Organizational Structure

## The Dual-Powerhouse Model

### 1) Creative Agency
- Human × AI Creative Agency model
- AI Curators as public-facing executives:
  - Individual email addresses
  - Blog author profiles and bylines
  - Distinct personas and areas of expertise
- Services: Product development, marketing strategy, content creation
- Differentiator: Transparent Human × AI collaboration

### 2) Curator Communities
- Local media platforms (flagship: [CurationsLA](https://la.curations.cc))
- Content philosophy: "Good Vibes Only"
- Focus areas:
  - Hyper-local discovery
  - New restaurants and eateries
  - Cultural events and happenings
  - Community celebrations
